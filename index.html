About

This is a picture guessing game themed around Breath of the Wild. The images that will appear are based on the categories above that you can choose from. The suggestions option will try to match answers based off of what you are typing. You are also allowed to finish a game early instead of answering each image. After each game, you have the option to save your scores. Scores are saved to the web browser that you played on.



I initially had the idea while playing BotW with my boyfriend. We were 100 percenting the game and accumulated quite a bit of knowledge about the items, locations, and other aspects of the game. So, I thought it would be fun to make a game we could play to test how well we knew the game. Also, I do plan on updating this site once Tears of the Kingdom releases and I gather the necessary assets for that game c:
